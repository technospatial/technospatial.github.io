Topographical & Utility Survey

Topographical surveys accurately capture the site-specific coordinates and height data for manmade or natural features and the surrounding terrain. The data is used to generate spot height maps, contour maps, or more complex terrain models of the surveyed area. Furthermore, understanding what utilities are present is vital when planning and undertaking both design and construction work. Working with the latest equipment to ensure the most accurate data is captured, our skilled staff conduct topographical surveys to meet the most rigorous and exacting specifications for your project.

The surveys we offer include: - 2D & 3D digital terrain modelling - GNSS surveys and control - Small and large scale mapping - Boundary surveys
